Rides

Ride 1

A fun ride off to the right of the main entrance.
It is coloured bright orange so there is no way you could miss it!
You must be at least 3 foot tall to go on this ride.

Ride 2

A fun ride off to the left of the main entrance.
This ride is quite gentle and doesn't have any height variation, making it ideal for children who are frightened of big rides.
No height restictions for this one!

Ride 3

A fun water ride located in the right of the waterpark.
You must be at least 3 foot tall to go on this ride.
